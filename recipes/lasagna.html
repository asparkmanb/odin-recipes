Lasagna

Description

This is a tasy lasagna. It is super easy to make and your whole family will love it. They will always request for this to made for Thanksgiving and family dinner.

This is also very easy to make. You will not need very many ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Mushrooms
  • 1 Green Pepper
  • 1 Onion
  • 3 Cloves of Garlic
  • 2 TBSP Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Cook the lasagna noodles in a large pot of boiling water for 10 minutes, or until al dente. Rinse with cold water, and drain.
  2. In a large saucepan, cook and stir mushrooms, green peppers, onion, and garlic in oil. Stir in pasta sauce and basil; bring to a boil. Reduce heat, and simmer 15 minutes.
  3. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Spread 1 cup tomato sauce into the bottom of a greased 9x13 inch baking dish. Layer 1/2 each, lasagna noodles, ricotta mix, sauce, and Parmesan cheese. Repeat layering, and top with remaining 2 cups mozzarella cheese.
